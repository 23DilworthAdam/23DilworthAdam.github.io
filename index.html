What Is 20-Time

The origins of 20-Time can be traced back to the 1950s where 3m started their 15% project. The results of their project were masking tape and post-its. The 20-Time project we know today was started at Google. At Google they decided to have their workers have a side project that they would spend 20% of their time on. The results of this project are Gmail, Adscence, and Google news.
